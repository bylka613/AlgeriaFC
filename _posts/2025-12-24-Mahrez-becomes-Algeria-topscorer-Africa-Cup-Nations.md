**With his brace today against Sudan, Riyad Mahrez became Algeria's all-time top scorer at the Africa Cup of Nations with 8 goals**

The Algeria captain opened the scoring in the 2nd minute of the match before adding a second early in the second half to take his all-time tally at the African Cup to 8 goals. Mahrez is now 18th all-time in the history of the competition. level with Pascal Feindouno (Guinea), Ahmed Hassan (Egypt), Seydou Keita (Mali) and Asamoah Gyan (Ghana).
