**With his 2 goals against Sudan on Wednesday, Riyad Mahrez became Algeria's second all-time top scorer with 36 international goals, level with Abdelhafid Tasfaout and behind only Islam Slimani.**

The Algeria captain opened the scoring in the 2nd minute of the match before adding a second early in the second half to take his all-time tally to 36 goals. Mahrez is now level with Abdelhafid Tasfaout, but still 11 goals behind Algeria's all-time top scorer Islam Slimani, who has 47 international goals.
